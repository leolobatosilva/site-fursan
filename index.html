Who We Are

FURSAN: Defense and Security specializes in institutional and commercial representation in the defense and security sector.

We act as a strategic link between companies, governments, and defense forces, maximizing opportunities and ensuring effective solutions.

Our operations cover Brazil and Latin America, strengthening our clients' presence in the market and enabling strategic projects.

The founders of the company have extensive expertise in institutional engagement at the regional level in Brazil, working closely with local, state, and federal security forces.

Additionally, they closely monitor and engage with the federal operations of the Armed Forces, including the Army, Navy, and Air Force, ensuring a deep understanding of defense policies and operational strategies.
